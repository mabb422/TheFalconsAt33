Active Hackbox

Established 1985

Every Spring Peregrine Falcons make their home in our nest box. Through a partnership with the Midwest Peregrine Society, the building's ownership placed what is called a "hackbox" on the eastern facing ledge (51st floor) of the office tower. This was done as part of an effort to repopulate the birds throughout North America starting back in 1985.
